Research

Finite element modeling for complex shell behavior.

Vaishali's doctoral work focused on four-node flat shell finite elements for static and free-vibration analysis of cylindrical, spherical, and conical shell panels made from composite, sandwich, and functionally graded materials. The research bridges formulation, meshing strategy, boundary conditions, validation, and interpretation of structural response.

A quadrilateral flat-shell element for composite and sandwich shell panels Static and free vibration analysis of sandwich shell panels Free vibration response of cylindrical and spherical FGM shell panels Four-node shell element for FGM spherical shell panels
