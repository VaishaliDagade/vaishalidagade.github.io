Research/FEA Summary

Ph.D. structural engineer focused on finite element analysis of composite, sandwich, and functionally graded shell panels. Research experience spans quadrilateral flat shell finite element formulation, static analysis, free vibration analysis, layered material behavior, cylindrical/spherical/conical shell panels, benchmark validation, and interpretation of structural response. Also brings 10+ years of practical structural design, formwork, steel, RCC, and construction-documentation experience.
